# Prompt 05 - Your Clips, Your Story (feeding the witness row)

Prereq: prompt 02 (or the Orbit rebuild from prompt 07). This is where the
kit stops being a demo and becomes YOUR set. Your clips, your rights.

---

Point yourself at my clip library at [YOUR CLIP FOLDER PATH] and help me
populate the empty rows of my composition with MY footage.

Method - curate first, load second:
1. Browse the library and summarize what I actually have: folders, counts,
   codecs, rough moods. Flag anything that is not DXV/HAP (slow codecs
   stutter live; suggest re-transcode with Resolume Alley, do not load).
2. Propose a manifest BEFORE loading anything: for each empty cell -
   exact file path, why it fits the column's energy (my columns are an
   arc: open, build, peak, comedown, null, finale), and transport mode
   (Timeline loop for textures; BPM Sync only for cut-to-beat material).
   The Body/witness row wants humans or instruments - the thing that
   makes the set yours. Show me the manifest as a table and STOP.
3. After I approve: load in batches of at most 8, wait 12 seconds after
   each batch, then ask me to confirm a save. Never skip the settle.
   If a load errors, stop the batch and read the cell's real state back
   before continuing - do not fire blind.
4. Pixel-verify: trigger two full columns and describe the composite
   (all layers visible? blends right? nothing black that should not be?).

Rules: never move clips between cells by drag logic (copy is safe, move
lies), never load anything I flagged as broken, and if Resolume crashes,
relaunch it, wait a full minute, and resume from the last save - the
batches-plus-saves discipline exists exactly for this.

VERIFY (me): trigger through the whole set with music. Every column
should read as a scene, and the witness row should read as a POV.
